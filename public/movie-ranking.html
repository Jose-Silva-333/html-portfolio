The Best Movies According to Zyxon

My top 6 movies of all time.

Wicked

Great adaptation from the musical. It's super fun and catchy. The story is really moving. It makes anyone like musicals. Or at least this one.

Avengers: Endgame

A great ending for this saga with a lot of entertwined movies and characters. A pop culture massive event on its realease.

The Mitchells vs. the Machines

Awesome animated movie with a distinct artstyle and really great representation of a family and its dynamics.

How to Train Your Dragon Franchise

I love this animation movies. Tons of amazing images and a thrilling story.

Big Hero 6

Great animation movie from pixar in partnership with Marvel. Features badass heroes.

M3GAN

Very interesting concept for an horror movie. A little goofy at times, but great.
